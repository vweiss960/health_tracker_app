GritBoard Music Player

Native Android App

A dedicated music player that runs natively on your Android phone. Unlike the web player, the native app keeps music playing when your screen is off and automatically skips songs that can't be played.

Features

  • Background playback — music keeps playing when screen is off
  • Lock screen & bluetooth controls
  • Auto-skip blocked or unavailable songs
  • Shuffle playback enabled by default
  • Generate Mix — describe a mood and AI builds a custom playlist
  • Play your saved GritBoard playlists
